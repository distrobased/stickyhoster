Customize Your Linux Experience

Explore desktop environments, icon themes, extensions, and tweak tools to make Linux truly yours.

🎨 GNOME Tweaks

Tool to adjust GNOME desktop appearance and behavior. Perfect for enabling themes, icons, and fonts.

🔧 KDE Plasma

A feature-rich and ultra-customizable desktop environment. Supports widgets, animations, and global themes.

🖌️ Icon Packs

Popular choices: Papirus, Tela, Qogir, BeautyLine. Easily install via your package manager or GitHub.

💻 Desktop Environments

  • GNOME – Modern and clean, default on Ubuntu
  • KDE – Highly configurable and polished
  • XFCE – Lightweight with decent visual control
  • Cinnamon – Familiar layout for Windows users

🧩 Extensions & Themes

Use gnome-extensions or extensions.gnome.org to enhance your DE.

🎯 Cursor Themes

Give your pointer personality. Try Bibata, Capitaine, or Nordzy cursors for a slick look.

🌗 Light & Dark Modes

Many DEs support dynamic switching. Some even allow scheduled theme changes at sunset/sunrise.

🧠 Window Managers

Feeling advanced? Try i3, bspwm, or AwesomeWM for total keyboard control and minimalism.

🖼️ Wallpapers & Compositors

Use feh, nitrogen, or variety to manage wallpapers. Pair with picom for transparency & blur effects.
