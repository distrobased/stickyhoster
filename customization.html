Customize Your Linux Experience

Make Linux look and work exactly how you want. Here are some ways to customize:

Desktop Environments

Choose from GNOME, KDE Plasma, XFCE, Cinnamon, and more for unique looks & features.

Window Managers

Lightweight and flexible alternatives like i3, bspwm, and Openbox for advanced users.

Themes & Icons

Use tools like GNOME Tweaks or KDE Settings to apply GTK themes, icon sets, and cursors.

Terminal Customization

Customize bash/zsh prompts with tools like Oh My Zsh, Powerlevel10k, and Nerd Fonts.

Boot & Login Screens

Modify GRUB themes and login managers for a seamless visual identity.
