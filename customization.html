Compare Popular Linux Distributions

Select the best distro for your needs. Here's how they differ:

Ubuntu

Beginner-friendly, stable, widely used, and backed by Canonical. Great for development & desktop use.

Debian

Rock-solid stability, slower updates, preferred for servers. Community-maintained.

Arch Linux

Rolling release, fully customizable, advanced users only. Arch Wiki is gold.

Fedora

Red Hat-backed, bleeding-edge software, GNOME by default. Ideal for developers.

Pop!_OS

Ubuntu-based with better hardware support & workflow features, great for gaming.
