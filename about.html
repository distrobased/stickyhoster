Our Mission

LinuxWorld is built with love to make Linux more accessible, exciting, and empowering for everyone — from beginners to power users, creators to casual explorers. We believe in open-source, in freedom, and in sharing what works.

Why We Started

Too many people think Linux is hard, scary, or only for coders. We’re here to change that with clear guides, fun comparisons, and a vibe that’s friendly — not formal.

Who We Are

We're a tiny crew of passionate users from Denmark & beyond, united by open-source love. Designers, nerds, creators, and keyboard tinkerers. We do this because we love it.
